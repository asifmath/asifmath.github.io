Philosophy & Approach

In my teaching, I aim to bridge the gap between abstract mathematical rigor and practical application. Whether I am teaching group theory or financial mathematics, I emphasize the "why" behind the formulas.

"My goal is to empower students to see mathematics not as a set of rules to memorize, but as a language to describe and solve real-world complexities."

I am particularly interested in developing interdisciplinary courses that connect pure mathematics with Quantitative Biology and Modern Finance, preparing students for the analytical demands of current research and industry.
